# Business Case

Name: SolarTech N'Djamena  
Location: N'Djamena, Chad  
Industry: Solar Energy Installation  

## Problem
- Frequent power outages
- High fuel costs for generators
- Lack of reliable electricity

## Solution
- Solar and hybrid energy systems
- Installation, maintenance, and monitoring

## Target Audience
- Homeowners
- Small and medium businesses
- Institutions (schools, clinics)

## Value Proposition
Reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable energy tailored to local conditions in N'Djamena.
