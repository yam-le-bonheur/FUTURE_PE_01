# Product Case

Product: Solar Home System (Panels + Battery + Inverter)  
Location: N'Djamena, Chad  

## Problem
- Frequent power outages
- High fuel costs for generators
- Unstable electricity for homes and businesses

## Solution
Affordable and reliable solar systems providing continuous electricity without fuel dependency.

## Target Audience
- Homeowners
- Small business owners
- Shops and restaurants

## Value Proposition
Save money, avoid outages, and gain energy independence with solar power.
