# Validation & Proof of Work

## Objective
Test the effectiveness of UGC-style ad scripts for solar product promotion.

## Approach
- Created multiple hooks targeting real pain points
- Designed short, engaging scripts
- Focused on emotional triggers (cost, frustration, reliability)

## Results
- Hooks are direct and problem-oriented
- Scripts follow a clear structure: Hook → Problem → Solution → CTA
- Content is adapted to local context (fuel cost, outages)

## Variation Test
Changed tone to more aggressive marketing:
- Stronger urgency
- More direct CTA

## Conclusion
The prompt successfully generates effective UGC ad content tailored for social media marketing in energy-related use cases.
